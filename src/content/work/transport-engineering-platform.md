## Challenge

Workshop operations, quote management, job tracking, and customer fitment records were spread across spreadsheets, email threads, and paper job cards.

Growing fleet-customer contracts and compliance obligations — mine-spec body certifications, hydraulic safety records, and ADR compliance documentation — demanded a single cloud platform with auditable workflows, role-based access for tradespeople and supervisors, and real-time visibility for fleet customers.

## Our approach

We delivered the platform end-to-end on Microsoft Azure:

- **Discovery and process mapping** with workshop supervisors to understand how jobs actually move through the shop floor.
- **Solution architecture** developed alongside fleet customers, so the system reflected real contractual and reporting needs.
- **Full-stack build** using Angular for an operator-friendly UI optimised for tablet use on the shop floor, and .NET for the job, quote, BOM, and compliance services.
- **Terraform-managed infrastructure** across AKS, Azure SQL, Blob Storage, and Application Gateway.
- **CI/CD via Azure DevOps** with environment promotion gates for repeatable, audited deployments.
- **Structured post-launch hypercare**, followed by a managed support retainer.

Data residency was kept within Azure Australia East to meet local compliance expectations.

## Outcome

The platform was delivered to production on schedule. It sustained **99.9% uptime over its first three months**, cut quote turnaround time by roughly **40%**, and gave compliance officers a single audit trail for body-build certifications.
