## Challenge

Altivision positions itself on a strong promise — "SAP careers are engineered,
not just taught" — but needed a website that could carry that message to fresh
graduates, career-switchers, and working professionals, and turn interest into
booked counselling sessions.

## Our approach

We designed and built a modern, conversion-focused website:

- **Clear course architecture** across SAP modules (SuccessFactors, FICO, MM, SD,
  ABAP, CPI and more), so visitors quickly find the path that fits them.
- **Outcome-focused messaging** around placement assistance, certification
  preparation, and real-world projects.
- **Testimonial-driven social proof** and prominent calls to action for booking
  counselling and enrolling.
- A **responsive, fast, SEO-ready build** optimised for the conversion-oriented
  flow typical of education providers.

## Outcome

The site, live at [altivision.co.in](https://www.altivision.co.in), gives the
institute a credible, conversion-first presence that channels prospective
students toward counselling and enrolment — and demonstrates the kind of website
work LoqalAds delivers for clients.
