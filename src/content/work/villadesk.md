## The problem

Independent villa and homestay owners in India lose money three ways: OTA
commissions of around 15% on repeat guests, enquiries that go cold because
replies are slow, and balance payments never collected after check-in. The tools
built for hotel chains are too heavy and too expensive for an owner running one
to fifteen properties.

## What we built

VillaDesk is a product we own and operate end-to-end — from design and
engineering to support and growth:

- **WhatsApp automation** for confirmations, reminders, and review requests, sent
  from the owner's own number.
- **UPI payment links** for advances and balances, so money is collected on time.
- A **10-stage booking pipeline** that makes sure no enquiry slips through.
- **Smart seasonal pricing**, **iCal sync** with Airbnb and Booking.com, and
  **GST invoicing**.
- A **daily 8 AM briefing** of check-ins and pending balances, and **caretaker
  logins** with financial figures masked.

## Outcome

VillaDesk is live at [villadesk.in](https://www.villadesk.in) with tiered plans
(Starter, Growth, Pro) and a 14-day free trial. It gives independent owners the
operational discipline of a hotel chain — without the cost or complexity — and is
LoqalAds' flagship product.
