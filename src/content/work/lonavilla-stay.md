## Challenge

Bookings were heavily dependent on third-party aggregators — MakeMyTrip, Airbnb, Booking.com — charging **15–20% commission per stay**.

The existing online presence was minimal: limited photography, no direct enquiry path, weak local SEO, and slow mobile load times. The villa was indistinguishable from larger StayVista and SaffronStays inventory in the same Lonavala–Khandala search results.

## Our approach

We designed and built a mobile-first brand website focused entirely on direct bookings:

- **High-impact photography** of the pool, garden, and rooftop terrace.
- An **enquiry-and-availability flow** integrated with WhatsApp Business and email.
- **On-page SEO** targeting high-intent Lonavala and Khandala keywords — private pool villa, group stays near Pune and Mumbai, weekend getaway villa.
- **Image optimisation** with lazy loading and CDN-served media for sub-2-second mobile loads.
- **Structured data** for property listings, plus Google Analytics and Search Console instrumentation to attribute enquiry sources.

It was deployed on managed cloud hosting with SSL, automated backups, and a lightweight CMS — so the host can update tariffs and seasonal offers without developer involvement.

## Outcome

The site reduced aggregator dependency and recovered roughly **15–20% per direct booking** previously lost to platform commissions. Mobile page-load times improved to **under 2 seconds**, direct enquiries rose by around **35%** in the first three months post-launch, and the villa ranked on page one for several long-tail Lonavala search terms.
